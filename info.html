Information

All of the proceeds from our creature collections are donated to animal shelters and reputable animal aid services. Our goal is to save critters from homelessness and cruelty. Each year, approximately 7.6 million animals enter animal shelters nationwide and nearly 3 million don't make it out. We are a charitable company we are partnered with Animals Of The World Wildlife Trust who help animals worldwide. Our products are also cruelty free, we would never harm a creature with our products.
